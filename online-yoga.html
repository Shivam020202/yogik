Getting Started

Everything you need to know to begin your online yoga journey

Technical Requirements

Stable Internet Connection

Minimum 5 Mbps for HD streaming, 10 Mbps recommended for live classes

Compatible Device

Computer, tablet, or smartphone with updated browser or our mobile app

Audio & Video

Built-in speakers/headphones and camera (optional for live classes)

What You'll Need

Yoga Mat

Comfortable Clothes

Props (Optional)

Quiet Space

Perfect Home Setup

  • 6x4 feet of clear space
  • Good lighting (natural preferred)
  • Minimal distractions
  • Water bottle nearby
