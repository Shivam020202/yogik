Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our yoga workshops

Bring a yoga mat, comfortable clothing that allows movement, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide props like blocks and straps, but you're welcome to bring your own. For hot yoga classes, bring an extra towel and dress in breathable fabrics.

Not at all! Our workshops cater to all levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Our experienced instructors provide modifications for every pose, ensuring everyone can participate safely and comfortably.

Regular classes are 60-75 minutes, while specialized workshops range from 90 minutes to 3 hours. Weekend intensives can be full-day sessions (6-8 hours) with breaks. Specific durations are listed in each workshop description.

You can cancel up to 24 hours before a class for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours receive a 50% credit for future use. Workshop cancellations require 48-72 hours notice depending on the event. Emergency cancellations are reviewed case-by-case.

Most workshops welcome ages 16+. We offer specialized teen classes (13-17) and family yoga sessions. Participants under 18 need parental consent. Some advanced workshops may have minimum age requirements that will be clearly stated.

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual workshop options. Online sessions include live streaming with interactive Q&A, recorded sessions for later viewing, and digital workshop materials. Check our schedule for virtual workshop dates.

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and bank transfers. Package deals and monthly memberships are available with automatic billing. Cash payments are accepted for walk-in classes subject to availability.

Please inform us of any injuries or conditions during registration. Our certified instructors provide modifications and alternative poses for all physical limitations. We recommend consulting your healthcare provider before starting any new exercise program.
